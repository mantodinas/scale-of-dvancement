Type II Civilization

Harnessing the total power of a parent star.

The Stellar Tier

A Type II civilization has graduated from planetary energy to capturing the 100% of the radiation emitted by its star. This equals roughly 1026 Watts.

At this stage, a civilization is essentially immortal. No planetary catastrophe—be it an ice age, asteroid impact, or super-volcano—can threaten their survival, as they have the energy to move their entire planet or even manipulate the star itself.

The Dyson Sphere

A massive shell or swarm of satellites built around a star to capture every photon of energy it produces.

Star Lifting

Removing plasma directly from the star to use as raw material for mega-engineering and fuel.

Stellar Engines

Using the star’s own radiation pressure to move the entire solar system through the galaxy to avoid cosmic threats.

Immortal Status

With nearly infinite energy, computing power and resource scarcity are solved, allowing for digital consciousness and deep-space habitation.

Technosignature Focus

We look for Type II civilizations by searching for KIC 8462852 (Tabby's Star) and other stars that show unusual "dimming" events, which could be massive structures passing in front of the sun.
