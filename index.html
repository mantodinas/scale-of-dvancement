The Kardashev Scale

Measuring the technological evolution of cosmic civilizations.

What is the Scale?

Proposed by astronomer Nikolai Kardashev in 1964, the scale is a method of measuring a civilization's level of technological advancement based on the amount of energy they are able to use.

As a civilization grows, its energy needs expand exponentially. The scale distinguishes three primary types based on the mastery of planetary, stellar, and galactic energy sources.
